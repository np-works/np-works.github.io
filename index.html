Me, Myself, & Korean Entertainment

About

Hi, my name is Nancy! I created this site to showcase how my love for Korean culture has developed over the years, starting from discovering KPOP to appreciating Korean food. Hope you enjoy my story:)

Discovering Kpop

In high school, I came across a Youtube video called "Teens React to EXO - Call me baby". Watching a kpop mv online for the first time was a very unique experience. I had never seen a genre of music that had dancing as a major component of a song and so watching these handsome guys dancing so in sync with each other was a pretty cool thing to discover. On top of that the music seemed very different than what I was used to with American music. As you can imagine, I started watching and searching for their other songs and fell into a deep hole of obsession. But it wasnt only their songs that were so attention grabbing, I later found that they had really fun and diverse personalities while watching their live TV specials. Pretty soon I became a true fan, waiting for their upcoming new song MVs every few months called "comebacks" and squeeling like a schoolgirl whenever my favorite member came on screen. Don't laugh, I was only 15. Mind you, I had never really cared about any American boy bands or ever was a Belieber so me getting all excited about a Korean boy band was out of my character. I've been a fan of EXO ever since.

Kdrama Binge

I got into kdramas after I found out that my favorite kpop singer was an actor. I looked up his previous works and found his drama It's, Ok That's Love. After that, I was hooked. I included my absolute favorite dramas below (in no particular order) for you to binge watch and enjoy.

Scarlet Heart Ryeo

Historical drama about a woman who travels back in time and meets different men who care for her in different ways.

Watch Now
It's Okay That's Love

My first drama ever! A novelist clashes with a psychiatrist on a talk show, but soon they become roommates.

Watch Now
Coffee Prince

My all time favorite drama. Story of an unlikely romance between a woman, who dresses like a man in order to get work, and a young rich man.

Watch Now
Itaewon Class

Inspiring drama. An ex-con and his friends fight to make their ambitious dreams for their street bar a reality.

Watch Now

My Kpop Dancing Career

Home

I first started to learn how to dance in high school after having watched so many Kpop group choreographies that seemed so fun. In secret, when noone was home, I would loudly play Kpop music and attempt mimicing the moves that I saw on the screen. I didn't progress that much in hs but I had the best time learning how to work with my body and groove with the music.

College

In college, I auditioned for a variety of Kpop dance clubs. I was obviously still very new to dance, but I got into a few of them and learned a lot from being on a team. After 3 years of dancing, I was finally able to move in a way that didn't look I was having a seizure. Check out our Youtube channel below!
